Rituals have been part of us as far as the beginning of our existence. Taking medications correctly and timely has itself become a medical problem. Medmana strives to be the best of the many apps available in providing simple tools in understanding and practicing medication adherence.

    Medmana aims to help you :

  • 1. With simple rituals and feedback
  • 2. Manage ordering prescriptions and refills
  • 3. With how to use and store meds - educational resources
  • 4. With social reinforcements in the form of social tips
  • 5. With Twitter feeds tailored to your meds and health conditions

What are the features?
Autocomplete for med names, milligrams etc, best time of the day auto reminders, report issues socially and to appropriate Federal Agency, keep a record of problems, twitter feeds of recalls and prescription receiving from doctor and transferring to a pharmacy(protecting your choice of a pharmacy).
